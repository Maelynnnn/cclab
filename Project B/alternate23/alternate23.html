LAB3 | Security level: 10

     Alternate's Human-based replicates have the same appearance as the parent human samples, are self-aware, and have shown aggressive behavior towards the parent human samples. With the exception of Alternate No.2 & Alternate No.3, all Alternate replicates have been destroyed.

     The cultivation of Alternate No.2 & Alternate No.3 has gradually exceeded LAB3's control, and we have decided to destroy these two samples in secret.
