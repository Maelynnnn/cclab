[ Satrick Star ]

Scientific Name:

[ Satrick Star ]

Discovered by / at:

Maelshaidsark.L, 2024 Spring

Family:

Satrick is from the Nocturnal Creatures family, the genus Star. Creatures in the same family include Moonisa, Planetia, and others.

Habitat:

Satricks have been observed in groups, and are only seen at night, floating back and forth in the night sky, occasionally stopping to rest and rotating their limbs when they are excited. Satrick will make the world go from night to day by spinning himself to emit light, but this process will also cause Satrick to gradually die until the next night, when new Satricks are born.

Appearance:

Satrck's body is very bright at night, has four tentacles that can float and move, and his brain is protected in the middle of his limbs.Satrick's limbs are very flexible, allowing him to swim and rotate at multiple angles. The smaller Satrick's body is red in color to protect itself and confuse its enemies. Larger Satricks have white bodies.

Key Characteristics:

  • Only live at night.
  • Sacrificing themselves to bring light
  • Will be blown away by the wind.
  • Flexibility of movement

What we know so far about [ Satrick Star ]? There is currently no way for a Satrick Star to successfully survive the Blackout, and even if they don't spin they will gradually die, which seems to be their fate. However, they are very fertile, with new Satrick Stars appearing with each new night, so there's no need to worry about them becoming extinct for the time being.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Don't touch Satrick Star! Since they live in the sky, their bodies are likely to be extremely hot, just watch them quietly. Respect the sacrifices made by the Satrick Stars, who gave their lives to bring light to the world.
